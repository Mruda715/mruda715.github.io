“Mama to Bee”

For a mother to be, baby shower, I created the invitations, game cards, thank you cards, centerpieces, and dessert decorations. All illustrations were made in Illustrator then transferred into InDesign for the layout of type and finalization. There were about 80 printouts for invitations, 25-30 dessert decorations, 120-150 game cards for each game, 100 thank you cards, and 10 centerpieces.
